Property description

Located just 250 meters from the beach in Bečići, one of Montenegro’s most picturesque coastal towns, the Galop residential complex offers a unique opportunity to own a modern apartment with stunning sea views in an elegant residential setting. Only a 5-minute drive from Budva, the famed “Queen of the Mediterranean,” this development combines comfort, prime location, and long-term investment potential.

Key Features:

  • Brand-new development with direct sea views from most of the apartments
    • Wide selection of residential units, including studio apartments, one-bedroom, two-bedroom, and three-bedroom layouts
    • Surrounded by shops, restaurants, and one of the most beautiful beaches on the Budva Riviera
    • No real estate tax for buyers – direct purchase from investor

The complex consists of four buildings: “Arched”, “Lineski”, “Tower”, and the “Apartment Building”. The complex will feature a swimming pool, underground garage parking, and commercial premises.

Avaliable three-bedroom apartments:

  • “ARCHED BUILDING”, B61, 6TH floor, 135.41m², PRICE 514,558€
  • “ARCHED BUILDING”, B64, 6TH floor, 134.18m², PRICE 509,884€
  • “ARCHED BUILDING”, B65, 6TH floor, 133.09m², PRICE 505,742€

Parking places in the underground garage are avaliable at an additional charge of 25,000€.

This location in Bečići offers excellent connectivity, with just 26 km to Tivat Airport and 61 km to Podgorica Airport. This complex presents an excellent opportunity, whether you’re seeking a personal coastal retreat or a profitable real estate investment, offering the perfect combination of location, lifestyle, and luxury.

Contact us today for more information or to schedule a virtual or in-person tour.
